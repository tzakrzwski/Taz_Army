Nintendo Switch

  • Relased: March 3, 2017
  • Sales: 17.79 million
  • Most Sold Game: Super Mario Odyssey
  • Ranking (in sales): 10th (for NOW)

The Switch is Nintendo's current flagship console. The Switch builds on Nintendo's other advancements, leading to a hybrid home and portable console. The Switch itself is a tablet looking device that can have joy-con remotes attached to both sides in order to create a portable game pad. The console can also be docked and the remotes removed to play on the big screen. All in all, the Switch takes the technology of the DS, the Wii, and Wii U and combines them. At the current time, the catalog for the Switch is not very large, mainly because of its recent release. However, the games that are currently released for the console, especially the first-party ones, are some of the best that the market has to offer and will most definitely become classics in the years to come.

Games

Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

  • Relased: March 3, 2017
  • Sales: 9.98 million
  • Rating: 10/10
  • Genere: Action-adventure

Nintendo has been making Zelda games for decades and still they never fail to innovative and keep their game-play at a high quality. Breath of the Wild is no exception as it takes the adventures and locations of Hyrule to a open world experience. The design of the whole world is beautiful and their are side quests and areas to explore around every corner. Also, the game adds a new game mechanics of constantly collecting and using breakable weapons, forcing you to quest to find powerful and interesting weapons. Another new game feature are shrines, which function as mini-dungeons. There are over a hundred of these and some require side quest just in order to unlock them. All in all this game is jam packed with contend that will keep you interested and excited every time you find something new.

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

  • Relased: April 28, 2017
  • Sales: 9.22 million
  • Rating: 9/10
  • Genere: Kart racing

This is a very fun and addictive game. It's good for the entire family to play together despite your skill level. It has 42 characters, 48 Grand Prix tracks, and tons of kart customizations! I especially like to play the new battle modes with my friends!

Super Mario Odyssey

  • Relased: October 27, 2017
  • Sales: 10 million
  • Rating: 10/10
  • Genere: Platformer, Action-adventure

Odyssey is THE Mario game that takes the series back to the open-world days of the Super Mario 64. This game does more than just that, as it has over 850 collectible moons that are scattered all over beautiful, well crafted worlds that make players want to hunt for each and everyone. This game is filled to the brim with areas to explore, challenges to conquer, collectibles galore, and hats, lots of hats. The controls for Mario in this game are incredible and his new hat companion allows him to jump high up in the sky. It can not be stressed enough the immense amount of content in this game and how every element of the game from the worlds, to the music, to the controls are perfectly crafted to make you fell like a child while playing this light and playful masterpiece.

Rocket League

  • Relased: November 14, 2017
  • Sales: 10.5 million
  • Rating: 8.5/10
  • Genere: Sports

Rocket League is a game in which you play as a car and play giant soccer against a variety of opponents. Seasons, Custom Battles, Basketball, and Ice hockey are all playable modes that you can play against the computer or friends. 1V1, 2v2, 3v3, and 4v4 battles are also available to accommodate the amount of people who want to play. This game allows you to customize your car to gain more surface area, speed, or better boosts to help the car. While it is hard to control the car at the start, when you learn how to play it is extremely fun.
